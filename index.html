How It Works: The Technical Explanation

Our formats specifically counter the increasingly common practice of students taking screenshots of exam questions to feed into AI tools. Here's how they defeat both direct scraping and screenshot-based cheating:

Audio Format: Defeating OCR and Screenshots

  1. 1 Screenshot-Proof: When a student takes a screenshot, the critical numbers aren't visible in the imageâ€”only the audio buttons appear, making OCR impossible.
  2. 2 Client-Side Generation: The audio is generated in real-time within the user's browser using speech synthesis, so there's no audio file for AI to extract or process.
  3. 3 Context Isolation: Without the numerical values, AI cannot understand the complete question context, making it impossible to provide accurate answers.

Data Reveal Format: Immune to AI Analysis

  1. 1 Transient Display: Values only appear briefly on hover/click and then disappear, making them difficult to capture in screenshots and forcing students to manually note them down.
  2. 2 Tooltip Architecture: The hidden values exist in a separate DOM layer that isn't captured by simple OCR or screen readers in their default state.
  3. 3 Multiple Interactions Required: By forcing multiple separate interactions to reveal all values, we create a process that's tedious to document for AI and disrupts the context flow.

Accessibility: Ensuring Equal Access

  1. 1 Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements can be accessed using keyboard-only controls with arrow key navigation and Enter/Space activation.
  2. 2 Screen Reader Support: We include hidden complete text versions that only screen readers can access, ensuring visually impaired users get the full content.
  3. 3 Reduced Motion Options: For users with vestibular disorders or motion sensitivity, all animations and movements can be disabled while maintaining functionality.
